Terasaki is a world class specialist in innovative circuit protection, control and system products for electrical energy distribution. We supply low-voltage circuit breakers for industrial, marine and commercial building projects. Terasaki has a highly qualified team of Application Engineers who offer our valued customers free technical support 24 hours a day. Our products and our people work with safety and protection as their prime purpose.
